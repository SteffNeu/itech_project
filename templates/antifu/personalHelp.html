Cyber Bullying

With the age of the internet there have come a lot of new ways of attacking people personally and in a hurtful manner. It is easier than ever to verbally attack and direct people. A mean facebook post or tweet is so easy to make and you don't even have to look the victim in the eyes and face the consequences. This new development has caused a lot of people to be victimzed and hurt. Cyber bullying is a serious problem in todays society. We here in Anti-FU work hard on this website to make people aware of hateful content and what influence it can have on peoples lives.
